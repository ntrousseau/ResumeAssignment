Noelle Rousseau

Web Developer and Graphics Specialist

Profile

Ms. Rousseau currently serves as a Graphics Specialist. She has a strong background in Graphic Design and Content Management and has worked in the Graphic Design field for over 10 years. Currently, she works with the Sister Study, a longitudinal cohort study that studies the role of gene-environment interactions in breast cancer risk where she fulfills graphic design and website development needs as well as coordinates/tracks printing costs with outside vendors. Among others, she also works with IFED-2 (Infant Feeding & Early Development) Study, NIDDK - Diabetes in America, PEGS (Personalized Environment and Genes Study), and InVitD Trial (The Investigation of Vitamin D and Menstrual Cycles Trial) on website development and graphic design requests.
